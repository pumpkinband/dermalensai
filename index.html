About DermaLens AI

DermaLens AI is a medical support application that uses artificial intelligence to analyze skin images and provide dermatology-related insights for informational purposes only.

The application may also facilitate chat-based consultations with licensed medical professionals. All outputs are advisory and must not be used as a substitute for professional diagnosis or treatment.

Medical Disclaimer

DermaLens AI is not a medical device. The application does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

  • AI outputs, overviews, symptoms, investigations, treatment options provided are informational only not to be mistaken for proper medical guidence
  • Predictions may be inaccurate or incomplete
  • Final medical decisions must be made by a qualified healthcare professional
